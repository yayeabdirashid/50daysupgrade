🥩 Protein Maalintii

  • 1.8–2.2g protein / kg miisaankaaga
  • Digir, beed, hilib, caano ah
  • Markii jimicsi ka dib: 30–40g protein
  • Oo kala qaybso 4–5 jeer

⚡ Kaloriye Xukumi

  • Calorie deficit yar: 200–300 kcal/maalin
  • Nafaqada xoogga ka saaro: rice, oats, potato
  • Jimicsi ka hor 2 saac: carbs fudud cun
  • Habeenkii: protein badan, carb yar

💧 Biyo

  • Ugu yaraan 3–4 liter maalintii
  • Jimicsi gudihiis: biyo oo 15 daqiiqo mar
  • Electrolytes ku dar haddaad si xoog ah jimicsi
  • Shaax, khamri etc. xaddi

😴 Nasasho & Hormoon

  • 8 saacadood hurdo: testosterone kor u kacaa
  • Cortisol hoos u dhac: is-nasto, stress xog
  • Nasasho maalinta ke murqaha waxay kordhaan
  • Qaado Magnesium habeenkii

Xeerarka Muhiimka ah

Ka dib ka dib si fiican
📈
Progressive Overload
Isboortiga kasta 1–2 asbuuc mar culeyska 5–10% kor u qaad. Murqaha kuma koraan haddaad waligaa culeys isla waydid.
🎯
Mind-Muscle Connect
Marka jimicsi sameyso, murqa meesha aad shaqeyneyso u fiirso. Caloolaha: oo tightened hayso jidba. Tani 6-pack-ka waa furaha.
⏱️
Nasasho Sets Dhexdiis
Xoog: 2–3 daqiiqo. HIIT: 30–60 sec. Calool jimicsiyada: 1 daqiiqo. Nasashada yar = lactic acid = murqo weyn.
🔁
Tempo Tartiib
Hoos u dhibaato 3 second (eccentric). Kor u kabo xoog 1 sec (concentric). Tani murqaha aad u kordhisaa faaidada.
🔥
HIIT vs Cardio
HIIT (sprint intervals) murqaha dhaawacdo yara waxay calool dufan gubaan traditional cardio ka siiso. Tani ayaanu isticmaalnaa.
💪
6-Pack Xaqiiqa
6-pack waxay muuqataa marka dufanka caloosha hoos u dhaco. Jimicsi calool + protein badan + calorie xukumi = natiijo muuqda.
